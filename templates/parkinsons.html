Energy Conserving Tips for Parkinson's Patients

  • Simplify your tasks and set realistic goals.
  • Plan your activities (chores, exercise, and recreation)ahead of time.
  • If needed, rest before and after activities.
  • Do not plan activities right after a meal. Rest 20-30 minutes after each meal.
  • Get a good night's sleep and elevate your head when sleeping.
  • If your doctor says it's ok, you may climb steps.
  • Avoid extreme physical activity.
